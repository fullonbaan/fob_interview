The Definitive Infor LN Interview Preparation Guide

Why Infor LN Interview Preparation Matters

Infor LN (formerly Baan ERP) is one of the most widely deployed enterprise resource planning systems for discrete manufacturing, with thousands of implementations worldwide. As organizations increasingly adopt CloudSuite Industrial (powered by Infor LN), the demand for skilled Infor LN consultants, developers, and administrators has never been higher. Whether you are preparing for an Infor LN developer interview, an Infor LN functional consultant position, or an Infor LN administration role, having a structured preparation strategy is essential to standing out from other candidates.

What Infor LN Interviewers Look For

Interviewers at companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Deloitte, Capgemini, and other major system integrators evaluate Infor LN candidates on several key competencies. For technical roles, expect deep questions on Infor LN 4GL programming, session development, form customization, report generation, and extension architecture. Administration interviews focus on system setup, security roles, batch job management, server configuration, and environment migration. Functional consultants must demonstrate expertise in specific modules such as Finance (General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable), Supply Chain Management (procurement, sales orders, warehousing), Manufacturing (production orders, shop floor control, MRP), Planning (enterprise planning, master scheduling), and Warehouse Management (inbound/outbound operations, inventory control).

Infor LN Technical Interview Topics

Our Infor LN Development interview guide covers critical technical areas including 4GL programming constructs (UI scripts, DLL programming, report scripts), session and form development lifecycle, data access layer and DAL functions, Enterprise Modeler concepts, BDE (Business Data Events) and Event Handling, server-side scripting, and customization best practices. The Infor LN Administration guide addresses system parameter configuration, multisite and multicompany setup, user authorization and role management, batch job scheduling, data migration tools, and server performance tuning.

Infor ION & BOD Integration Interview Questions

Modern Infor LN implementations rely heavily on Infor ION (Intelligent Open Network) for integration and Infor BODs (Business Object Documents) for enterprise messaging. Our dedicated Infor ION interview guide covers ION Desk configuration, integration flows, API gateway setup, document flows, connection points, and troubleshooting integration failures. The Infor BOD interview guide addresses BOD structure and schemas, message processing, enterprise integration patterns, and real-world scenarios for connecting Infor LN with other Infor CloudSuite applications.

Infor LN Functional Interview Topics

For functional consulting roles, interviewers test your understanding of end-to-end business processes within Infor LN modules. The Finance interview guide covers chart of accounts design, financial company structure, period processing, bank management, and financial reporting. Supply Chain Management questions address purchase order lifecycle, sales order processing, warehousing operations, and logistics management. The Manufacturing guide covers item management, production order types, shop floor control, material requirements planning (MRP), and capacity planning. Planning module questions focus on enterprise planning, order planning, master scheduling, and demand forecasting within the Infor LN environment.
